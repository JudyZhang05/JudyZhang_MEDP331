Chapter I - My Reading Addiction

"A reader lives a thousand lives before they die . . . Those who never read lives only one" - George R.R. Martin

How It Began

My first book that got me addicted was borrowed to me by my brother. The book was a recount of the atrocities committed in WW2 and it's painful aftermath, which has scarred an entire nation and left it in shambles. I didn't quite expect to enjoy the book as much as I had. Additonally, the speed in which I had completed the book in has shocked and motivated me to read more.

It's a Lifestyle

Reading has by far been the one entertainment that I have enjoyed most in. Each turn of a page brings a new journey and adventure.

I still consider myself a beginner when it comes to reading, I have only dipped my toe in the world of books and am always on the lookout to read more and explore other genres.

Self Control

Some tips on when you start reading. Have some self control. Don't be like me. See a new book + immediate purchase of entire series + prioritize reading over daily necessities + unhealthy. Don't enhale the entire book in one sitting. What are you? A sociopath? Savor the book , let the plot sit and stir you randomly throughout the day. That's the beauty in reading, it should be a prolonged mystery as to what will happen next.
